About


Welcome to OPM, your friendly space for everything about Philippine music! Whether you’re a fan of modern OPM, curious about our cultural musical roots, or simply love discovering Filipino artists, this site is made just for you. Here at OPM, we celebrate the rich and diverse world of Filipino music — from classic kundiman and traditional instruments, all the way to today’s viral hits and breakthrough artists. We believe that music is one of the strongest parts of our identity, and we’re here to share its beauty with everyone.


What We Do


OPM brings together a fun mix of content, including: Stories behind your favorite OPM songs Spotlights on talented Filipino artists and composers Discoveries from different genres—from folk to pop, rock, R&B, indie, rap, and more Features on Philippine music history, culture, and traditional sounds Recommendations, fun facts, and music inspiration Whether you're exploring for learning, nostalgia, or pure enjoyment, we aim to make your visit both informative and entertaining.


Why We Created This Page


Our mission is to help people appreciate Philippine music by making information about its genres, history, artists, and cultural roots easy to understand and enjoyable to learn.


  • Show different kinds of music found in the Philippines.
  • Explain how Philippine music has changed from past to present.
  • Encourage pride in Filipino culture and creativity.

Feel free to browse around, discover something new, and enjoy the rhythm of Filipino music with us. We’re always excited to welcome fellow music lovers — let’s keep the passion for Pinoy music alive together!
